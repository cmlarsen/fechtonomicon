# Support for The Fechtonomicon

Welcome to The Fechtonomicon support page! Here you'll find answers to common questions and ways to get help.

## About The Fechtonomicon

The Fechtonomicon is an educational flashcard app for studying Historical European Martial Arts (HEMA). It features terminology and techniques from legendary masters like Joachim Meyer, presented in a beautiful medieval-manuscript aesthetic.

## Getting Started

### How to Use the App
1. **Swipe through flashcards** - Swipe left/right to navigate
2. **Tap for details** - Tap any card to see full information
3. **Use the widget** - Add the widget to your home screen for quick learning
4. **Select disciplines** - Go to Settings to choose which systems to study
5. **Search terms** - Use the search tab to find specific techniques

### Adding the iOS Widget
1. Long-press on your home screen
2. Tap the "+" button in the top left
3. Search for "The Fechtonomicon"
4. Select your preferred widget size
5. Tap "Add Widget"

The widget updates automatically throughout the day with random flashcards.

## Common Questions

### How do I reset my progress?
Your progress is stored locally on your device. To reset, simply delete and reinstall the app.

### Can I suggest corrections or improvements?
Yes! Tap the pencil icon (✏️) next to any editable text field to submit corrections. Your suggestions will be reviewed and may be incorporated into future updates.

### Does the app work offline?
Yes! All content is stored locally on your device. You don't need an internet connection except for:
- Watching embedded YouTube videos
- Submitting correction suggestions

### Which disciplines are included?
Currently:
- **German Longsword** (Joachim Meyer, 1570)
- **Italian Longsword** (Various masters)

More disciplines are planned for future updates!

### How do I hide cards I've already studied?
The app automatically tracks which cards you've viewed and prioritizes showing you new cards. Once you've seen all cards, the cycle starts over.

## Troubleshooting

### The app isn't opening
- Try restarting your device
- Make sure you have the latest version
- Delete and reinstall if issues persist

### The widget isn't updating
- Check that the widget is properly added to your home screen
- The widget updates hourly - wait a bit and check again
- Try removing and re-adding the widget

### Videos aren't playing
- Check your internet connection
- Some videos may be region-restricted by YouTube
- Try opening the video link in Safari if available

### I found incorrect information
Please submit a correction using the pencil icon (✏️) in the app, or contact us directly at caleb@yetanothersidequest.com

## About Historical Sources

The content in The Fechtonomicon is based on historical treatises and modern HEMA interpretations. We strive for accuracy but interpretations may vary. For detailed study, we recommend:

- **Wiktenauer** - https://wiktenauer.com
- Your local HEMA club or instructor
