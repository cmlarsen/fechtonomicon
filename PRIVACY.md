# Privacy Policy for The Fechtonomicon

**Last Updated:** November 9, 2025

## Overview

The Fechtonomicon ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in relation to the App.

## Data Collection

**The Fechtonomicon does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All data related to your use of the App is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

## Local Data Storage

The App stores the following information locally on your device:
- Your study progress (which flashcards you've viewed)
- Your selected disciplines
- Your quiz scores and history
- App preferences and settings

This data is stored using:
- **iOS**: UserDefaults and App Groups (for widget functionality)
- **Local storage only** - never synced to the cloud

You can delete all this data at any time by deleting the App from your device.

## Third-Party Services

### YouTube Embedded Videos

The App contains embedded YouTube videos for educational purposes. When you play a video within the App, YouTube may collect data according to their privacy policy. We do not control YouTube's data collection practices.

- YouTube Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### GitHub (Optional Edit Suggestions)

If you choose to submit a correction or edit suggestion using the in-app feature, your submission is sent through GitHub's API to create a pull request. This is entirely optional and only occurs when you explicitly choose to submit a correction.

- GitHub Privacy Policy: https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement

## No Analytics or Tracking

The App does not use:
- Analytics services
- Tracking cookies
- Advertising networks
- User behavior monitoring
- Crash reporting that collects personal data

## No User Accounts

The App does not require or support user accounts, login, or authentication. You can use the App completely anonymously.

## iOS Widget

The App includes an iOS home screen widget that displays flashcards. The widget:
- Only accesses data stored locally on your device
- Does not transmit any data
- Uses App Groups to share data between the main app and widget extension
- All data remains on your device

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else. The App is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, your data security is controlled by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, etc.).

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to:
- Provide to you
- Correct or update
- Delete from our servers

All your data is on your device and under your control.

**In short:** The Fechtonomicon is a privacy-first app. Everything stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your data. Your study progress is yours alone.
